GREY WATER MANAGEMENT AND REUSE FOR WETLAND MANAGEMENT AND WATER CONSERVATION:

*Grey water management is the process of collecting, treating, and reusing wastewater from bathrooms, wash basins, washing machines, and kitchens (excluding toilet water). This treated water can be reused for irrigation, gardening, wetland maintenance, and other non-drinking purposes, helping to conserve fresh water.

Components Required:

1.Arduino Uno

2.Water Level Sensor



3.Relay Module

4.DC Water Pump

5.Filtration Tank (sand, gravel, charcoal)

6.PVC Pipes

7.Storage Tank

8.Jumper Wires

9.Power Supply

Objective:

1.Reduce freshwater consumption.

Applications:

1.Homes

2.Schools and colleges

2.Parks and gardens

3.Agricultural irrigation

4.Wetland restoration projects
